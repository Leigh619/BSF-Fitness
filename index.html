Welcome to BSF FItness

As a community of fitness enthusiasts we hope to provide a community that allows anybody from any background and understanding to learn how to achieve their fitness related goals. Foundationally, this fitness server was created from passion coming from a small group of people who just loved all things fitness. Created by the founder Leigh, along with others such as Tom, Clemens, Davey, Mel, and Harry it started off small and grew over time through during the COVID-19 pandemic in September 2020. 5-years, and with ups and downs within the community we still strive to help individuals who are looking to transform their physique, mindset, and physical capabilities with over 3,0000 members.

What does our community offer?

Our community offers a diverse range of people from all different educational backgrounds, and countless number of experienced gym goers. Because we care more about quality than quantity in our server we have multiple people with bacherlor degrees, master degrees and PhDs in the server. Some covering nutrition, sports science, strength & conditioning, personal training, medical science, physiotherapy and more. You can therefore lots of information from professionals in the world of fitness in this server.
